NeonSmp Official Rules

Last updated: 5 August 2025

Welcome to NeonSmp Community. These rules are in place to ensure a fun and fair environment for everyone across our Network and Discord.

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in a mute, kick, or ban from server or Discord depending on severity of offense.

Click to join NeonSmp Support server on Discord.

Minecraft Server Rules

Network Wide

The following behaviors are not allowed on server.

Prohibited Actions

  • Spamming or flooding of chat.
  • Disrespectful behavior towards staff or players.
  • Using hacked clients or unfair advantages.
  • Advertising other servers.
  • Exploiting bugs or glitches for personal gain.
  • Griefing or stealing in Survival modes.
  • Scamming players in trades.

Lifesteal Mode Exceptions

In Lifesteal, some rules regarding combat and theft are adjusted. However, Toxicity remains banned everywhere.

Discord Server Rules

Community Guidelines

Keep our Discord a safe space for everyone. Generally, if it is not acceptable in-game, then it is not acceptable on Discord either.

Chat Conduct

  • Non-English speech is not allowed in public channels (keep it to English).
  • Spamming, mass pinging, or excessive caps.
  • Disrespectful behavior or harassment.
  • Do not share NSFW content.
  • No racism, sexism, or discriminatory language.
  • Do not impersonate staff members.
  • Do not send malicious links or files.
  • Respect channel topics (e.g., no support questions in general chat).
  • No begging for ranks or items.
  • Do not argue in public channels; take it to DMs.
